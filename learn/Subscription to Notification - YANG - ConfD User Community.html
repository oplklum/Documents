          Hi pomishra,

          I believe your issue simply is that you did not enable the notification capability for NETCONF in your ConfD configuration file, i.e. confd.conf.

            <netconf>
...
    <capabilities>
...
      <notification>
        <enabled>true</enabled>
      </notification>
    </capabilities>
  </netconf>
